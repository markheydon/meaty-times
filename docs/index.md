Meaty Times is a free, simple cooking assistant for roasting joints of meat. Tell it what you are cooking, how much it weighs, and how you like it done — and it gives you clear oven temperatures, cooking times, resting guidance, and optional serve-at scheduling so your roast is ready when you want to eat.

[Try Meaty Times](https://webfrontend.yellowcoast-79687552.ukwest.azurecontainerapps.io/)

## Why use Meaty Times?

- **No accounts** — open the calculator and get answers straight away.
- **Clear instructions** — step-by-step temperatures, phased cooking where needed, resting time, and total preparation time in one view.
- **Food-safe defaults** — poultry, pork, and gammon use fully-cooked guidance; you are not asked to pick an unsafe doneness.
- **Plan around your meal** — optional backwards scheduling from a target serving time.
- **Works on any device** — designed for phones, tablets, and desktops.

## What you can do

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Roast calculator** | Select meat type, enter weight in kilograms, choose doneness where it applies, and calculate instructions. |
| **Two-phase roasting** | For beef and lamb, get a high initial temperature then a reduce-to step when the recipe calls for it. |
| **Resting time** | Every result includes how long to rest the joint before carving. |
| **Serve at planner** | After calculating, enter when you want to serve and see clock times for when to start, change temperature, remove from the oven, and begin resting. |

Supported meats in the current release: **beef**, **lamb**, **pork**, **chicken**, and **gammon**. Measurements use **kilograms** and **degrees Celsius**.
