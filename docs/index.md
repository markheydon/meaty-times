# Meaty Times

Meaty Times helps you roast meat with confidence. Choose a meat, enter its weight, and get clear timings, suggested temperatures, and a serve-at plan for the day.

![Preview of the Meaty Times roast calculator]({{ '/assets/images/meaty-times-preview.svg' | relative_url }})

## Why people use it

- Friendly roast guidance for beef, lamb, pork, chicken, and gammon
- Clear timing calculations that help plan dinner around the clock
- Food-safe temperature guidance with doneness options where relevant
- A simple, mobile-friendly experience for everyday cooks

## What you can do

- Pick a meat type and weight
- See recommended cooking times and target temperatures
- Calculate when to start cooking for a planned meal
- Use the app on desktop or mobile
